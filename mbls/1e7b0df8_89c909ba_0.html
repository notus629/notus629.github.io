<Location> & <LocationMatch>

    若匹配 Location 或 LocationMatch 对应目录, 则执行 section 内部的指令


    示例

        <Location "/image">
            Alias "/ftp/pub/image"
        </Location>

        <LocationMatch "/error/(?<NUMBER>[0-9]+)">
            Alias "/usr/local/apache/errors/%{env:MATCH_NUMBER}.html"
        </LocationMatch>
