Commandline Arguments

    up-to-date documentation

        $ man memcached
        $ memcached -h



Most important arguments

    -m    in megabytes, tells memcached how much RAM to use, at least 64 M

    -d    tells memcached to daemonize.

    -v    controls verbosity (显示信息) to STDOUT/STDERR. Multiple -v's increase verbosity.
