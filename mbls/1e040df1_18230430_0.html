<Directory>

    限定对某个目录级的配置

    <Directory> and </Directory> are used to enclose a group of directives that will apply only to the named directory, sub-directories of that directory, and the files within the respective directories.


    示例

        # Deny access to the entirety of your server's filesystem
        <Directory />
            AllowOverride none
            Require all denied
        </Directory>


        ## Regular expressions can also be used, with the addition of the ~ character
        <Directory ~ "^/www/[0-9]{3}">
            ...
        </Directory>
