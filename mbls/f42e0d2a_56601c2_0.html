GnuPG

    GnuPG (GNU Privacy Guard, GPG)

    GnuPG is an Open Source alternative to the well-known Pretty Good Privacy (PGP) by Phil Zimmermann.

    Most Linux distributions ship with GnuPG installed by default.

    To verify the signature for a specific package, you first need to obtain a copy of our public GPG build key, which you can download from http://pgp.mit.edu/.
