RewriteRule - Basics

    A RewriteRule consists of three arguments separated by spaces.

        1. Pattern: which incoming URLs should be affected by the rule; (regular expression)

        2. Substitution: where should the matching requests be sent;

        3. [flags]: options affecting the rewritten request.
