Redirect

     Redirect preserves path information. A redirect for a URL /one will also redirect all URLs under that, such as /one/two.html and /one/three/four.html.

        ## redirect URLs under /one to http://one.example.com
        Redirect "/one/" "http://one.example.com/"

        ## Redirect http URLs to https
        <VirtualHost *:80>
            ServerName www.example.com
            Redirect "/" "https://www.example.com/"
        </VirtualHost>



RedirectMatch 

    Allow you to include a regular expression in your redirection criteria
