Universal USB Installer 

        Aka UUI, Opensourced software, Runs on Windows XP/Vista/7/8/10 or WINE.

        A Live Linux USB Creator that allows you to choose from a selection of Linux Distributions to put on your USB Flash Drive.

       Your USB drive must be Fat16/Fat32/NTFS formatted, otherwise Syslinux will fail and your drive will NOT Boot.
